## Getting Started

### Accessing the Platform
Log in with your administrator credentials. Upon your first login, a **Welcome Tour** will guide you through the main interface.

### Account Setup
Ensure your profile details are correct in the **Settings** tab. This information may appear on quotes and invoices.

---

## Dashboard Overview

The **Dashboard** is your central command hub.
- **Top Stats**: Instantly see Active Events, Pending Quotes, and Monthly Revenue.
- **Upcoming Events**: A timeline view of what's on the horizon.
- **Recent Activities**: A real-time feed of system updates (e.g., new inquiries, quote approvals).
- **Quick Action**: Click `+ New Event` in the top right to start planning immediately.

---

## Managing Events

The core of the platform. Navigate to the **Events** tab to view your calendar.

### Creating an Event
1. Click `+ New Event`.
2. Enter the Event Name, Date, Guest Count, and Client Details.
3. The system will create a new Event Record.

### Event Details
Once created, you can manage specific aspects of an event:
- **Menu Planning**: Assign dishes and calculate costs.
- **Staffing**: Assign chefs and servers.
- **Run Sheet**: Create a timeline for the event day.

## Menu Management & AI Parsing

We've simplified menu entry with AI.

### Uploading a Menu
1. Go to **Menus** > **Upload**.
2. Drag and drop a PDF or Image of your menu.
3. **AI Parsing**: The system automatically reads the file, extracting:
   - Dish Names
   - Descriptions
   - Prices
   - Dietary Tags (e.g., GF, V)
4. Review the extracted items and click **Save** to add them to your database.

### Inventory & Costing
Each menu item is linked to ingredients. The **Cost Calculator** helps you estimate the profit margin per dish based on current ingredient prices.

## Staff & Scheduling

Manage your team effortlessly.

### Staff Directory
View all your chefs, servers, and bartenders.
- **Availability**: Check who is free for upcoming dates.
- **Roles**: Assign specific roles (e.g., Head Chef, Server).

### Scheduling
1. Open an Event.
2. Go to the **Staffing** tab.
3. Select available staff members.
4. The system sends them an email notification (if enabled).
